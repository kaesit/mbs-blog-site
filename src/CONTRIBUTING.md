# How to Contribute?

Hi! This file will be your guide while your contributing journey.
Please follow the rules, be humble, authentic and carrying.
We don't want to involve with politics if it isn't a social science topic, please remind these things to yourself while using website and treat other people nice in comments or review sections. 


# Rules
- No racism, hate crime towards to anybody or anything, only the topic you want to discuss
- While you contribute you are going to face with tags if your content is not fit for the qualifications for particular tags that you have checked, your content will not be published
- It is not expected from you to be an expert the area of your contents but we won't welcome spreading misinformation, conspiracy theories, warmongering or any other thing will effect society in a negative way(Criticizing war crimes and genocides are not included in this matter).
-  Feel free to share your thoughts with other people by not crossing their red lines and also be open to report other people who is not following rules and violating the platform

## Content Types
There is types of contents what they includes and means that will help you.

**Full Content**:
Full content means it literally consist of every type of contents which includes
 - Videos
 - Articles
 - Blog
 - Photos
 - Documentation
 - Project Files
 
 **Videos**:
 Videos consist of video files that has no limit for long, you can share your videos about any tag anything and make them related to a primary content title.
 
  **Articles**:
 Articles are consist of real researches and seriously formatted documents.
 Publishing an article is different from sharing a video, blog or repo, it might take weeks to get an approval. We don't just see articles as "contents" as other shareables on our platform.
 
 **Blog**:
 Blogs are not have to be scientific as articles so if you had some cool stuff to talk, share or discuss you can easily share blog and communicate with people and discuss your thoughts.

 **Photos**:
Photos are photo contents like mobile platforms as you are similar, fast, less space and available.
**Documentations**:
Documentations are actually designed to be  a part of projects but they can also be shared for educational purposes, awareness guidelines not just for coding or science projects.

**Projects**:
Many of people knows project repos nowadays, if you are similar to this topic,
it helps you to store or share your projects, we do not provide an version control system yet but we will soon. If your project is in a primary content(full content) with articles, documentations, videos and photos your content will be highlighted more than others. 


## Switch to another file

All your files and folders are presented as a tree in the file explorer. You can switch from one to another by clicking a file in the tree.

## Editing a content

You cannot specifically just change a whole content but you can edit.
Here is the list of thing you can edit:
 - Misspell letters
 - Content title
 - Project title
 - Photo titles
 - Placeholders

## Delete a content
We're not storing your deleted content for 7 days or 90 days like other platforms, we just delete, so if you press **Delete** button don't forget you cannot undo.
